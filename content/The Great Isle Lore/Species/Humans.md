Humans are, as any fantasy world, the default humanoid species in the continent. They are everywhere there is sentient life, and are by far the most common humanoids. 

They are known for being rather short lived, versatile and, in general, very adaptable. Due to their short lives, they tend to be ambitious and even brash to the eyes of other longer lived species. 

While they are not over other species in certain abilities, the great amount of humans in the world ensures that there is, somewhere, a human that surpass most of individuals. For example, a dwarf is most likely sturdier than a human, but there is surely a human that is sturdier than most dwarves.
