Species are the different types of humanoids (also called "men" or "humans" in a general sense) that inhabit the Mortal Realm and other Planes.

**Common Species**: means that all species described in the Player's Handbook are present in the place. Most of the time, among the species the [[Humans]] are the most common; then [[Dwarves]], [[Elves]], [[Gnomes]], [[Halflings]] after; [[Tieflings]] and [[Orcs]] being rather rare; and [[Aasimar]], [[Dragonborn]], [[Goliaths]] the less common.
