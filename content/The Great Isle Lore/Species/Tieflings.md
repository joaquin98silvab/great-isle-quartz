Humanoids touched by abyssal, cthonic or infernal influences, they are known for their colorful skins, horns and tails, reminiscent of their fiendish ancestors. While evil touched their blood at some point of their or their ancestors lives, they have no more affinity to evil, violence or manipulation than any other humanoid. Almost all tiefling sons and daughters are also tieflings. Their origin determines many of their characteristics:  

**Infernal:** by far the most common tieflings. More than a millenium ago, devils planted their influence in many humanoids, birthing the first tieflings. For many years they were hated and enslaved, but nowadays they are fairly common and no longer live the judgement of their ancestors. Most of them have red, blue or white skins and completely black or deep red eyes, many smell like brimstone, ash or coal. Their horns tend to be curved, many times similar to rams or goats. 

**Abyssal:** fairly new, these tiefling started appearing after the fall of the empire. They are the result of the profane ritual where a newborn's heart is replaced with a demon's by a demonic cult. The child is changed into an abyssal tiefling, and while they have no more tendency to evil than any other person, the circumstances around their childhood tends to produce hard individuals. They have light grey, purple, green or yellow skins, dark eyes and a large scar in their chest, product of the demonic ritual. Many of them sprout more than one pair of hard, straight horns.  

**Cthonic:** rarest, they are the offspring of a Succubus or Incubus that falls in love with a mortal. Love being so antithetic to a fiend existence, the Succubus in question is transformed into a fey-like being, their evil apparently cleansed. They look similar to their parent, of purple, pink, green or crimson skin, intense eyes and many smell like flowers, plants or things of nature. Their horns appear more similar to antlers, though they cant be shed and regrown.
