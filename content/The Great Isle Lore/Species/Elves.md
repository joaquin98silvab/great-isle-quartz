The elven people have mysterious origins and strange affinities to magic and the natural world. They are known for their extremely long lives (~800 years), slender frames and pointy ears.

Elves have a strong relationship to their environment. Their long lives is a reflection of nature, and so they themselves take the characteristics of their homeland. Thus, there are many different subspecies of elves in the continent, the most common being:

**Sun elves**: copper skins and golden or red hairs, their connection to the heavens gives them a close affinity to magic. They live in cities, hills and plains. They live in most places, but are most common in Aguta.

**Moon elves**: white to bluish skin, black, white and pale yellow hair, their connection to the heavens gives them a close affinity to magic. They live near mountains, in valleys, close to rivers and lakes. They are common in many countries, but most of all Dor Gaiar and Iurd.

**Wood elves**: brown to greenish skins, brown hair ranging from almost black to yellows, reds and greens. They live in large forests, and they tend to absorb the characteristics of their home woods. They are common in Dor Gaiar, but most large forests have at least one wood elven community.

**Dark elves or drow**: light purple to black skin, white or gray hair. They live in dark places, mostly in the underground. Most drow come from the shrouded city of Nurzenkal. They were only allowed to leave their city if they did subterfuge works for their empires, so they were believed to be shady assassins or thiefs. Now this stereotype is basically disproven, and some drow are found most places.  

**Sea elves**: blue and green skins, dark green hair, fins and gills. They live in most oceans, breathe water and are more comfortable under than over the sea line.

**Desert elves**: light to dark yellow skins, brown to black hair. They are sturdier than most elves, and live in wastes and deserts.

**Eladrin**: they come from the feywild, the parallel world of fairies, emotion and excess. Their appearance and magic is determined by their season, a reflection of their emotional state. Winter eladrin have white, silver and light blue colors, autumn reddish and orange, summer deep yellow, and spring greens and reds. They come from the feywild or places close to it, like Karim.
