Similar to [[Elves]], dwarves also take the characteristics of the environment they inhabit, but while the former are slender and gracious, the latter are stout and strong.

They tend to inhabit underground mines or fortresses, carved into the earth itself, and tend to be all over the continent, but mostly where there are a lot of hills or mountains.

Both male and female dwarves tend to have long beards and hair, and they are very different to tell apart by other humanoids.
