
There are no Greater Gods other than the original 10. All the rest are Ascended.

Ascended are non-godly beings (also called mortals) that are given godly powers by one of the 10 original gods. 

For example, [[content/The Great Isle Lore/Gods/Lesser Gods (Ascended)/Altar]] was originally an angel in the service of the greater god [[Leor]]. He was called to the mortal realm to cleanse the earth from the [[Infernal Incursion]]. Due to his work, he was rewarded by Leor and Ascended to godly status, becoming one of the most important gods in the realm, with more followers than most of the greater gods.
