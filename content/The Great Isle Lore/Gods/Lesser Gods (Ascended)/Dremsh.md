### The Second Eye
#### (Chaotic Good)

The second eye of [[Gruumsh]], however, was not destroyed by [[Corellon Larethian]]. It healed and regained form, but seeing what the old god had become into, turned into a god by itself. Dremsh took what was left of the old god that wasnt anger, becoming the patron of freedom, crafts, foraging and roaming. He sheltered the orcs from their creators fury, and let them roam free. Many orcs, nomads and travellers follow Dremsh, escaping the domination of Gruumsh.
