### Overview

He was the twin brother of [[Corellon Larethian]], but in a fight his brother took one of his eyes out. This enraged Gruumsh, becoming the god of hatred fury and strength, and taking his sons and daughters, the orcs, with him. To this day, some orc tribes follow Gruumsh, destroying and conquering.
