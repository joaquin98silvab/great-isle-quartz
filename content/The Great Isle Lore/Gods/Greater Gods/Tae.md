### The Night Moon
#### (Chaotic Good)
![[Pasted image 20250624104718.png]]
God of freedom, nature, love, the moon. God of moonlight, the Moon, the night and stars.

##### Common Appearance

A blue robed woman, many times sitting on the moon. Tends to carry a silver shield and curved blade sword. 

##### Patronage

Lovers, many druids and monks, good lycanthropes. Patron god of Dor Gaiar, and many other elven communities. 

##### Divine Domains

Nature, Trickery, Grave, Tempest
