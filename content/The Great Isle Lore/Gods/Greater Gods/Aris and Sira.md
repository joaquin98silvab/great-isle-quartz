### The Mothers of Life and Death
#### (True Neutral)

![[public/The-Great-Isle-Lore/1714c52c676790be426fee2bc7dbbcfd.webp]]
It is believed that Aris and Sira were the creators of the universe. One, the goddess of Life and beginnings, the other, goddess of Death and endings. Together, they are the goddesses of existence itself.

Although they are the goddesses above all gods, most people don't know they exist. They do not communicate nor grant anything to their followers, so they have no clerics or priests or cults. 


##### Common Appearance

They are represented as twin sisters, one with black skin, eyes and hair, clad in white robes, the other with white skin, eyes and hair, clad in black robes. Their hairs are braided together, and they appear to be infinitely long, merging with many colors until they meld into reality itself.

##### Patronage

Patrons of grave tenders, healers and druids, though they have almost no worshippers.

##### Divine Domains

None, they do not grant powers to their worshippers
