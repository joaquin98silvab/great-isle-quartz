### The Mad One
#### (Chaotic Evil)
![[Pasted image 20250624102822.png]]
God of murder, torture and senseless pain. 
##### Common Appearance

A woman with broken arms and legs, a wicked smile and broken bonds, covered in blood.
##### Patronage

Murderers, blood cults. Her cult if prohibited in many places, and her name and symbol is considered a bad omen.
##### Divine Domains

Death, Trickery, War, Blood
