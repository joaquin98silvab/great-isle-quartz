Head of the now defunct Ira Empire, Altar has a messy history. Still trying to escape a 1000 year old imperial past, Altar continues to be one of the most advanced and powerful kingdoms in the present.

**Culture:** late imperial spain, religious zealotry included

**Known for:** the Ira Empire, its Universities, its magic, the angel-god Altar.
