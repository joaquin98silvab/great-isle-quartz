The elven kingdom of Dor Gaiar is both a reflection of the mystical past and a politically powerful present. Crucial in defeating the Empire, it has gained power but not much territory, being a rather slow-acting and closed kingdom.

**Culture:** fantasy elves

**Known for:** its military, its old culture, its ports
