The dwarven "sea fortress" is a city state in the river mouth of the Greyed River. Almost impenetrable, has a treaty with Greyedbass to control the entrance to the river, while taxing the trade.

### Politics

The city of Uzgathol is more than a great-clan, but a whole union of seven different clans:

- Brewbelly
- Minefoot
- Broadback
- Oakspine
- Fortfingers
- Salthammer
- Purestone
- Leadheart

While all clans are, in paper, equal in dignity and societal standing, tradition dictates that a Salthammer couple should be the great-clan leaders. For the last four years, Narhola and Hovric Salthammer have lead Uzgathol.

In general, the Salthammer clan enjoys important military and government positions. Likewise, the Purestone clan is known for its worship of [[Shanee]], goddess of purity, and the Oakspines of [[Moradin]], the All-father.
