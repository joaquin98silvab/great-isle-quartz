High priest of the cult of [[Shanee]] in [[Uzgathol]]. In truth, he is the leader of the [[Cult of Ulgul]]. He receives guidance from Ulgul as he is the only one appart of [[Nermuni Purestone]] to be able to enter Ulguls cage. 

He once entered the cage goaded by Ulgul and his mind was shattered.

When the seepage got worse, he secretely killed the high priestess, [[Barduna Purestone]], telling everyone that she was not to be disturbed in her chambers, praying for Shanees intercession. 

He is manipulating the church and the clan leaders to thwart the hunt against the cultists. 

He uses a secret magical elevator in his bathroom to enter the cult headquarters, where he is creating a magical circle to channel [[the Dead Moon]] magic for creating the rot.

He manipulated the church into beleiving that killing the infected and sending their corpses into the sea and canals will help. In reality, he is strengthening the seepage.
