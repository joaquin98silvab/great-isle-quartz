He is a wererat. Now looks like a middle aged broad fat human man. In wererat form he looses all his fat, and his face looks more rat like.

He was part of the cult of [[Ulgul]]. They have been trying to open the gate of the Cage of Purity, where he is kept. Three years ago, they managed to start to open the Cage, allowing his influence to seep through. This attracted the attention [[Shanee]], the goddess of purity. To open the gate, the cult sacrificed many of its members, Thevenin (at this point called Jugelm Leadheart) included.

Shanee reincarnated Jugelm into Thevenin, and in exchange she made him focus the hole in the cage in the Starmap Gate, and made him escape into obscurity. He took the place of Thevenin, killing him, and taking the Gate with him. 

He was tasked with taking care of the Gate, not opening it. But the plague rats reached him, and started influencing him, making him repeat the mantra of not opening the gate so he doesnt open it.
