To look for the parrot, they can find out about a large travelling menagerie shop called The Emerald Saddles. 

**The Emerald Saddles:** owned by the couple Gelgel (short beard, blond, neat and orderly) and Gaterlun Amberhorn (scraggly beard, black, charismatic and showy). 
Common parrot: 1 gp
Dwarf silver parrot (knows dwarvish, can learn common): 3 gp
Translating parrot (can translate between deep speech and dwarven, but nobody knows deep speech): 20 gp
Other exotic birds 5gp
Dogs: 1 gp (10 gp trained)
Cats: 1 sp
Sporeling: 30 gp
Winged flightless cat: 30 gp
Octogenpus: 30 gp
Air breathing fishes: 20 gp (30 gp for the piranha)

Seeing the bad smell the menagerie decided to leave aboard the Lamellar (capt. Lorbumi Cavebrand).

They find out that the menagerie is leaving in the ship.

They manage to get into the Lamellar where they buy the Translating parrot, they call him Tako (means parrot in Deep Speech). It looks like a parrot with three feet, three longue tongues, a floppy beak, everlooking eyes, purple floppy feathers with kind of flowing or moving patterns and black dots. It eats once a day something that is living and drenched in water.

Bralmuni Oakspine is a young dwareven girl that helped the guys to enter the Lamellar. She got one silver for info about somebody that knew about forbidden knowledge.

They are led by Gardobella to an entrance of the sewers controlled by the Lowminers. They get into a fight with them.
