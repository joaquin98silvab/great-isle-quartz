The party starts in a small town called Lemane, in [[Junier]]. They were called by the Prophet in a letter that appeared next to their beds. They had to meet in Lemane, the last Sunday of March.

They meet in the Widow's Barrel Inn.

There, a local, Marten Balandeux, delivers a message:


> [!text] Quote
> "You are looking for me. Find me where the gods live and where a god lays dead. Find me where gods reign, and where they are imprisoned. Find me where a god wakes, and where it slumbers. I will be waiting, for it is fated."

After that, the Geas that was cast into Marten dispels, and he looks confused.

Short after, goblins attack (2 minion, 2 warrior, 2 boss)

![[goblin boss.png|340]] ![[goblin minion.png|340]] ![[goblin warrior.png|450]]

During the fight, they see goblins entering through the back door and windows, and the only escape being the trapdoor that the barman (Thevenin Leblanc) escaped into.

There, they find a secret path into a chamber with Thevenin and an artifact. The artifact looks like a hand clock or a compass, closed, with some kind of star map engraved. He mutters

>[!text] Thevenin
>"The gate, it must not be opened, do not touch it, its mine, it must not be opened. He shall be free, Ulgul shall be freed from his cage. And the gate must not be opened."

He coughs and spit to rat swarms, and he becomes a wererat.
![[swarm of rats.png|340]] ![[wererat.png|340]]

If the compass is opened, all the constitutents of the room are transported to the sea in front of [[Uzgathol]]


---
Old Jambert, the grandpa of Thevenin,  he is the oldest half elf in the town. He likes wine.

They killed the goblin boss. and went down Thevenin's hatch, where they find him with the Starmap Gate.
