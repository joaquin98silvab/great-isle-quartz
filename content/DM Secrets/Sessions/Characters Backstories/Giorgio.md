**Marco**

Giorgio was a pirate from [[Ventana]], with his crew of nakamas. They where caught and hanged for piracy. 

The prophet saved Giorgio after death, but the process left a part of Giorgio's soul detached from his body, turning into his astral self.

He now looks for answers about The Prophet.
