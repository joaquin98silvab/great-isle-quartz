**VIC**

He was from [[Confederation of Free Islands]], a rather rebellious child, he found a purpose and community in his sailing crew. He spent years with his second family, sailing the northern seas. 
Everything changed one night with a storm, stronger than any he had seen. They fought the sea with all their might, reaching the eye of the storm, a supernatural calm in the middle of the tempest. There, thunder came. Like rain, lightning fell down all around the ship. The crew, confused and wary, waited for the strange flurry of lightning to pass. They had angered the God of Storms, some said. 
One last lightning fell over the ships deck, though it did not light any fire. Instead, an old, robed man appeared. He asked for PC, and when he saw him, he just said "Your fate calls you, live not in chains".
After that, fire and thunder erupted from the sky, but not over the water, but the deck itself. People died all around him, burned and struck by lightning, and the ship was completely destroyed. Only one person survived, him, with a name in his head, The Prophet.
We washed ashore out in the beach of [[Eltrankardem]]. There, he was sheltered by a comunity of dragonkin, who taught him the art of the Drakewardens. When he was ready, he bonded a new drake, just an egg for now, and left looking for only one thing, revenge.
