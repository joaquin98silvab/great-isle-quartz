
Weapon (dagger), uncommon (requires attunement)

Runes dance along the blade of this keen knife.

**Sacrificial Knife**. While attuned to it, you can use this magic, rune-inscribed dagger as a spellcasting focus.

**Ceremonial Sacrificial Knife**. While holding the greater version of this dagger, you can use it to perform a sacrificial ritual during a short rest. If you sacrifice a Tiny or Small creature (starting from the size of a large rat), you regain one expended 1st-level spell slot. Once per day.
