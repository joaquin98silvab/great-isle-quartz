**Nikos**

He was from [[Nurzenkal]], with a good childhood. However, when he was only 27 years old (elves are considered adults when reaching 100), a strange man, The Prophet, approached him and asked him to lead him to the closest shrine of the Dead God. Jonik led innocently the Prophet to one of the very fingers of the God, four meters tall, and exuding power and protection. Horrified, Jonik saw how the Prophet carved with a shining blue knife into the Dead God, and collected a glistering golden liquid from the wound. The finger shriveled and broke of from the body. 

Paralyzed by fear, Jonik was caught by the authorities, and the Prophet nowhere to be seen. He was branded as the worst possible criminal, a Sacrilegious Exile, one who damaged the very God that protected Nurzenkal. 

Exiled, Jonik spent almost a century working as a subterfuge Bounty Hunter, always looking for the man that destroyed his life.
