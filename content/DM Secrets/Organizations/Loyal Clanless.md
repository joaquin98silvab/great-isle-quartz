The clanless dwarves of [[Uzgathol]], social pariahs that live in the sewers. They conduct a silent war against the [[Cult of Ulgul]]. They want to reintegrate into dwarven society.

Petty thieves, scammers, rogues, people that have shamed their clans in a great way. The dwarven honor rules are extremely complicated, but most of them didnt do really big crimes. The real criminals are executed by the loyal.

Their leader is [[Yoholim Clanless]]
