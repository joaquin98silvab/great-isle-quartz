Cult of [[Ulgul]].

Headquarters in the sewer caves. In the center, a magical circle channeling [[the Dead Moon]] power to create the rot. 

[[Lenodon Purestone]] can do a ritual. Killing a normal mortal, it creates the maggots. Killing a lycanthrope can permeate and worsen the rot. When he sacrifices himself, he can become a quasi avatar of Ulgul, containing divine power and following his commands, though not his own power nor his will.
