Tumultuous times came after the fall of [[The Ira Empire]], starting an era of political and cultural change. Now, 70 years after, things seem to calm down. The [[Dragon War]] has started to become a thing of history, if not legends, and the great evils appear eradicated.

There is no real peace, however. A mysterious man, [[The Prophet]], walks the earth causing tragedy and miracles in equal measures, and strange necromantic energies appear out of the heavens. 

There are always mysteries in the Great Isle, and always adventurers that look for answers.
